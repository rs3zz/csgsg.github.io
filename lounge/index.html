Lounge

Now available to graduate students in the CS/CpE department is the Graduate Student lounge (Rice 536) every week from Wed through Fri, 12 pm to 2 pm. Feel free to meet up with friends (real or imaginary) for lunch, have philosohical discussions ("What is the meaning of life?"), or find zen (if you believe it exists) as you take a break from the gruelling working hours of the day.

Location: Rice 536

Days: Wed-Fri (every week)

Time: 12 pm - 2 pm

P.S If you have suggestions on how to make this lounge more graduate student friendly (like adding board games, etc.) please drop us an e-mail and we will make it happen! :)
